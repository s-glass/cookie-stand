About Us

Welcome to Pat's Salmon Cokies! A familly business since 2022, Pat Patterson decided to take his love of baking and his passion for salmon preservation to create his famous Salmon Cookies. Proceeds from each sale go to the Save Our Wild Salmon Coalition.
